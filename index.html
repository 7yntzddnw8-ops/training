How a session runs ▾

16 min EMOM — 4 rounds, one minute each: Push → Legs → Pull → Core. On the minute, do the listed work, then rest the rest of the minute. Every exercise lands four times across the 16 min. Keep 2–3 reps in reserve.

3×10s = three holds inside the minute. 45s = accumulate across the minute.

Cardio first, every day — pick by time / space / weather: a 3–5K run or 10 min HIIT jump-rope.

Tap any line to check it off. The card turns green when all five are done, and the day tab gets a tick. Progress is saved on this device. Travel workouts are reference only.

Weeks follow the calendar. Week 1 begins 22 Jun 2026; the app opens on the current week automatically. Days (1–5) go on whichever working days the week allows.

Difficulty steps up each cycle; reps step up each week. Don't chase the next progression until the current one is clean. Deload whenever you need it — a missed day beats a tweaked shoulder.
